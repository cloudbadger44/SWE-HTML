Delicious Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Milk 1/2 cup
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
Baking powder 1 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. In a mixing bowl, combine flour and sugar.

  3. Add eggs, milk, and vanilla extract to the dry mixture and mix well.

  4. Stir in baking powder until the batter is smooth.

  5. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  7. Let it cool before serving.
